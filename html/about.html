About the Ministry of Health

The Ministry of Health of Trinidad and Tobago is responsible for ensuring the highest standard of healthcare services and public health protection across the nation.


Our Goal

The Ministry of Health is a proactive, people-centered and compassionate organization that makes sure all parties involved meet high standards in order to advance, safeguard and enhance the health of Trinidad and Tobago's citizens.


Our Objective

By emphasizing evidence-based policymaking, planning, monitoring, evaluation, collaboration and regulation, we hope to effectively lead the health sector. The Ministry of Health sets national health priorities and creates an environment that supports a variety of public and private providers to deliver high-quality and people-centered services.


Our Fundamental Principles

The following essential principles direct us in fulfilling our vision and mission:

  • Professionalism: We guarantee that skilled and qualified healthcare professionals provide the most effective and efficient delivery of medical services.
  • Total Quality: Our dedication to providing top-notch services and healthcare systems.
  • Evidence-Based: Dependent on studies and data-driven choices at all levels.
  • Visionary: Taking the initiative to lead the industry.
  • Client-centeredness is the provision of healthcare services that are sensitive to the requirements and preferences of the client.
